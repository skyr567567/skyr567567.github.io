We are Humanists Iceland

Humanism is a philosophical stance that emphasises the cerebral agency of humans, and reposes confidence in logic and humanity.

  • Become a member of Siðmennt!

Ethical humanism

Siðmennt is an organisation centred around ethical humanism. Ethical humanism bases it's believes on moral values and knowledge derived from reasoning and evidence.

Ceremonies

Siðmennt offers non-religious ceremonies for humanists and others who wish to honour important events in one's life. 

Religious freedom

Siðmennt believes that the government should be required to be neutral when it comes to religion, and not give preferential treatment to one religion.
